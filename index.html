Baseline

A regulation and social learning system for people with alexithymia, autism, and social anxiety.

Baseline helps you understand your internal emotional state, practice expressing emotions through facial cues, and interpret others' emotions — all in a private, judgment-free space. Use it before or after difficult social situations to regulate, reflect, and grow.

Breath Measuring Device Instructions: 1. Connect to power source 2. Tie around body so front and back are parallel 3. Breath out fully and press button to calibrate 4. Breath in and out in time with the boxed breath device 5. Both green lights should glow simultaneously.

Breath Guidance Device Instructions: 1. Turn on the device 2. Breath in starting from the continuous blue lights 3. Hold breath through the white lights 4. Breath out through the blue lights 5. Hold breath through green lights 6. Repeat.

Who this site helps

Baseline is designed for people with alexithymia, autism spectrum disorder, and social anxiety — as well as anyone who wants to better understand and regulate their emotional responses.
